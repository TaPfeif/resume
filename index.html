PROJECTS

Camera based pose estimation for balance rehabilitation

Pose-estimation system to support treatment of vestibular disorders.

As part of my Master's thesis, I built a prototype to assess balance workload during a one-leg standing test. The prototype is based on the MediaPipe pose estimation model and was evaluated with technical verification and clinical validation. I received the best grade for this project.

Non invasive glucose sensor

Prototype sensor concept for non-invasive glucose monitoring.

During an exchange semester at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, I helped develop a non-invasive glucose sensor. Tasks included printing of the sensor, electrochemical modification, and evaluation in terms of sensitivity and storage stability.

VegEx App for Home growers

Mobile app to help small-scale growers manage crops.

As part of the Global Sustainability Challenge, I developed a prototype for a social network for home growers. The platform allows home growers to exchange experience about harvesting from home, motivate each other, and most importantly trade harvests with other home growers. I achieved first place in the Asia Pacific Semi Finals.

vHIT quality testing

Tooling for quality assessment of vestibular head-impulse tests.

As part of my studies, I conducted a special course at the Copenhagen Hearing and Balance Center. I investigated the reliability of vHIT goggles used for the head impulse test. My research focused on finding out if the performed head rotation angle affected the gain calculation of the vHIT system.

RESETTLE clinical study

Clinical study focusing on rehabilitation outcomes.

As part of my studies, I contributed to the clinical study RESETTLE about obesity treatment of people with childhood obesity using semaglutide. I quantified body composition and correlated it to treatment success.

Surface discoloration detection

Computer vision to detect and quantify surface discoloration.

As part of my Bachelor's thesis, I conducted a study about surface discolorations from reprocessing. I developed an algorithm to quantify surface discoloration with the aim of researching how reprocessing intervals affect the amount of discoloration.
